# Coffee-Shop-Sales-☕
 
# 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive
Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard built to analyze sales performance, 
customer behavior, and product trends. 
It helps in making data-driven decisions by visualizing key business metrics in a clear and engaging way.



# 🎯 Objectives
Analyze total sales and customer footfall

Understand hourly order trends

Identify top-selling products

Compare performance across store locations

Study category and size distribution of orders


# 📌 Key Metrics
Total Sales: ₹6,98,496

Total Footfall: 149,116

Average Bill per Person: ₹4.68


# 📈 Dashboard Features
⏰ Quantity Ordered by Hour
Peak hours: Morning (8 AM – 10 AM)
Shows customer purchasing patterns throughout the day



🥧 Category Distribution
Coffee dominates with 38% sales share
Other categories include Bakery, Drinking Chocolate, and Tea


🥤 Size Distribution
Regular (31%), Large (30%), Small (9%)
Helps understand customer preferences


📍 Store Location Analysis
Compared Astoria, Hell’s Kitchen, Lower Manhattan
Hell’s Kitchen shows slightly higher sales


🏆 Top 5 Products
Barista Espresso leads in sales
Followed by Brewed Chai Tea and Hot Chocolate


📅 Orders by Weekday
Highest orders on Friday & Thursday
Lowest on Saturday


# 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard creation

Excel / CSV – Data source

Data Cleaning & Transformation – Power Query

# 💡 Insights & Learnings
Morning hours drive maximum sales


Coffee is the most preferred category

Weekdays generate more revenue than weekends

Store location impacts performance significantly
